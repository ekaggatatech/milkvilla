About Us

Milkvilla is a hyperlocal supply chain network with direct access to primary producers and consumers.

We are a decentralized network of farmers and consumers where primarily milk collection and delivery happen at the doorstep in non-packaged form.

We have completely digitalized the whole milk supply chain and drastically reduced leakage and processing with the help of technology. We are currently based out of Muzaffarpur district in Bihar.

We are expanding pan-india using owned and franchised centers.

Our Promise

Pure

Milk is tested at farmer's doorsteps and remains hand's free for the entire operation.

Non-processed

No processing is done on the milk. No chemicals or preservatives are added.

Non-packaged

No plastic packaging is done. Get milk directly into your container.
