About Us

Welcome to MilkVilla.in – Your Trusted Source for Fresh Dairy Goodness At Your Doorstep!

At MilkVilla, we are passionate about providing you with the finest, freshest dairy products that not only nourish your body but also delight your taste buds.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to connect farmers and consumers with pure, unadulterated milk and dairy products.

Milkvilla is a hyperlocal supply chain network with direct access to primary producers and consumers.

We are a decentralized network of farmers and consumers where primarily milk collection and delivery happen at the doorstep in non-packaged form.

We have completely digitalized the whole milk supply chain and drastically reduced leakage and processing with the help of technology.

We are currently based out of Muzaffarpur district in Bihar.

We are expanding pan-india using owned and franchised centers.
